Wikimedia Discovery timeline

The Wikimedia Foundation has recently focused on the development of a "search and discovery" tool, previously dubbed the Knowledge Engine. Lack of communication with the editing community, poor transparency, and conflicting statements about this undertaking have plagued the project, and unrest has been visible not only within the editing community, but within the Wikimedia Foundation itself.

May 1, 2014

Lila Tretikov joins as Executive Director

Lila Tretikov

Thirteen months after the March 2013 announcement that Sue Gardner was planning to step down as the Wikimedia Foundation Executive Director, the Board of Trustees appoint Lila Tretikov to the position.

  • Press release: Wikimedia Foundation names Lila Tretikov as its new Executive Director
  • Wikimedia blog: Announcing our new Executive Director: Lila Tretikov
  • New York Times: Open-Source Software Specialist Selected as Executive Director of Wikipedia
August 6, 2014

Media Viewer and superprotect conflict

The Wikimedia Foundation roll out the Media Viewer, a software feature intended to provide a "more immersive multimedia experience". Following a community decision, a volunteer administrator disables the feature. He is reverted by a Wikimedia Foundation staff member, who threatens to remove his administrator status. Soon after, the Wikimedia Foundation rolls out a software change called "superprotect" that restricts adminstrators from changing pages. These events result in an arbitration case on the English Wikipedia, as well as controversy on the German Wikipedia.

  • Signpost: Media Viewer controversy spreads to German Wikipedia
  • Media Viewer arbitration case
October 29, 2014

Damon Sicore joins as Vice President of Engineering

Damon Sicore

Damon Sicore joins the Wikimedia Foundation as the Vice President of Engineering. Before coming to the WMF, he worked at Edmodo and, before that, at the Mozilla Corporation.

  • Wikimedia blog: Damon Sicore joins Wikimedia Foundation as Vice President of Engineering
April 2, 2015 (not public until February 10, 2016 Signpost article)

Earliest document presented to the Knight Foundation

The Wikimedia Foundation finishes a document to present to the Knight Foundation as the motivation for a grant to support the search and discovery efforts.

  • Signpost: New internal documents raise questions about the origins of the Knowledge Engine
June 5, 2015

2015 Wikimedia Foundation Board of Trustees results announced

Dariusz Jemielniak (User:Pundit), James Heilman (User:Doc James), and Denny Vrandečić (User:Denny) are elected to the three two-year-long community seats on the Wikimedia Foundation's Board of Trustees.
